# Trading Rules Reference

Use this file when the user asks for concrete RSI or execution rules.

## Beginner-safe RSI framing

RSI is a filter, not a buy/sell button.

### Preferable zones

- Long bias: RSI 50–60
- Short bias: RSI 40–50

### Caution zones

- Longs in RSI 60–70: only after confirmation, not on emotional chasing
- Shorts in RSI 30–40: only after confirmation, not on emotional chasing

### Avoid as a beginner default

- chase longs with RSI > 70
- repeatedly buy dips just because RSI < 30
- repeatedly short just because RSI < 30 and price looks weak
- trade neutral RSI 45–55 with no structural reason

## Execution discipline rules

Use rules like:

1. No re-entry within 120 seconds after a trade closes.
2. No trading for 5 minutes after a loss if the urge is to “make it back”.
3. Stop after the first rapid flip inside a stressed window.
4. Stop after the second reactive trade inside the same 10-minute window.
5. In RSI < 20 or > 80 conditions, allow at most one experiment, not repeated attempts.

## Position building rules for beginners

Prefer:

- one idea = one position
- or at most two planned entries

Forbid:

- adding because the first entry is losing
- pretending emotional averaging-down is “scaling in”
- adding repeatedly in extreme RSI zones
